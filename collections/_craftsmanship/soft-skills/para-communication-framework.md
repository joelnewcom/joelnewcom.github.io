The **PARA method** is a simple framework for structured, engaging conversations. It keeps you focused and invites the other person to participate.

---

## The four steps

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **P**oint | Clearly state your main idea or the point you want to make. |
| **A**ction | Explain what action you took based on that point or situation. |
| **R**esult | Describe the outcome or impact of that action. |
| **A**sk | Pose a question to encourage the other person to share their perspective. |

---

## Example

> **Point:** "I've been realizing how important rest is for productivity."
>
> **Action:** "So, I started taking my weekends completely off to recharge."
>
> **Result:** "My work quality has improved significantly, and I have more energy during the week."
>
> **Ask:** "How do you manage your own rest and productivity?"
